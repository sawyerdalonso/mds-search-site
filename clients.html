Engagement models

The right structure for the stakes of the role.

The model changes. The standard does not: clear ownership, calibrated outreach and disciplined execution.

Retained search

For mandates where control and depth matter most.

Best suited to leadership-critical, confidential and highly specialized searches that require a comprehensive view of the market.

  • Dedicated search strategy
  • Structured market mapping
  • Ongoing calibration and reporting
  • Close and reference support
Contingency search

For important roles where speed and flexibility lead.

A performance-based model for searches that benefit from fast access to qualified talent without an upfront retained commitment.

  • Focused candidate outreach
  • Fast profile delivery
  • Payment tied to placement
  • Direct founder involvement

How a search runs

A clear process creates better decisions.

The work is visible, the feedback is direct and the search adjusts as the market teaches us more.

Kickoff + calibration

Define the success profile, compensation, interview plan and outreach story.

Targeting + outreach

Map priority companies and engage relevant active and passive talent.

Assessment + shortlist

Evaluate fit, share market signal and refine the funnel quickly.

Close support

Navigate references, offer strategy and candidate communication.
