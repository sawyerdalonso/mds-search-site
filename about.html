Meet the founder

Search experience, delivered directly.

Howard Braithwaite is the Founder of MDS Search and a trusted advisor to founders, executives and leadership teams during critical growth moments. With more than 20 years in executive search and talent recruitment, he has partnered with high-growth startups and established organizations to deliver C‑Suite, VP, Director and highly specialized individual contributor hires.

Before launching MDS Search, Howard spent 15 years with one of North America’s most respected recruiting firms, leading complex searches across Technology, Engineering, Manufacturing, Finance & Accounting and Biotech. He also built and scaled multiple million-dollar, revenue-producing recruiting and sales teams.

His recent work includes Tax, Assurance & Advisory placements for public accounting firms.

Known for a hands-on, high-touch approach, Howard brings market insight, tight execution and clear communication to every search while maintaining the confidentiality clients and candidates expect.
